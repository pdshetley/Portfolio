Everything is design

I think design should make people happy. I like people and like to discover what makes them happy. Whether through words, images, web, or print, the end of the means should make people happy. I think good design makes the world a better place. It can promote justice and speak truth. I bet it can even bring peace to the world if we tried a little harder.

My Work

I am Patricia Shetley

You can call me Patricia. I grew up on the East Coast riding horses, playing field hockey, and spending more time in high school art class than was needed. I got a BFA in graphic design while consuming excessive amounts of hoagies, bagels, and pizza. I am now gluten intolerant. I love all things made by hand including pixels. The great thing about pixels is that they can be anywhere, anytime, and available to almost everyone. Isn’t that great?
