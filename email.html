I designed emails for Botanica, a line of organic botanically-based skincare products. One email announces their big summer sale and the other asks users to subscribe to Botanica’s blog. I started with rough sketches to determine content such as the main offer, a place for the offer code, picking up assets from the website, and action buttons for the user to either start shopping, learn more, or sign up. A free tote bag is offered with every blog subscription.
